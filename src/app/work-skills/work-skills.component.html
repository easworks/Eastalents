INSTRUCTIONS - WORKSTYLE ASSESSMENT (WSA)

The WSA is based on DISC based assessment which are extensively researched, reliable, accurate and time-tested to determine how individuals see themselves responding to workplace situations that they perceive to be favorable or challenging, and reveals whether their response patterns are active or passive

It’s really an assessment and NOT A TEST. We use assessments to learn about a perspective or way of thinking. It has no right or wrong answers. You cannot pass or fail the assessment.

Answering these 28 questions only indicates a person's view or perspective. There is no way to get it wrong. And it takes as little as 7 minutes to complete.

RESPONDING

  1. Study the first group of four words on the WSA - DISC based Assessment while thinking about yourself in your WORK Environment or WORK settings.
  2. Answer honestly and spontaneously
  3. Select only one word that MOST describes you by checking the box in the MOST column.
  4. Select only one word that LEAST describes you by checking the box in the LEAST column.
  5. Use the same procedures to respond to the remaining groups of descriptive words.

REMEMBER: Select only one MOST and one LEAST choice for each group.
