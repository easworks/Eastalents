Overview

EASWORKS is a platform that connects a network of thoroughly vetted enterprise software experts with top companies and startups. Our primary goal is to ensure that we find & hire the most suitable EAS technical talent on-demand to give our client’s projects the highest chance of success.

EASWORKS talent is another term for “freelancer.” EASWORKS may interchangeably use the terms “freelance,” “EASWORK talent,” or “Expert” or “EAS Talent” to describe the members of our EAS Expert Talent Network. An Expert is an individual that is skilled in one or more of our work categories. Experts perform specific tasks related to work assignments. Experts work with their Customer Success Manager and the Customer. Experts who work as flexible talent work closely with the Customer to agree upon weekly or monthly deliverables and set expectations. EASWORKS talent contracts generally track their time hourly for each client and each contract.

Our Customer Success Managers (CSM) work as the main point of contact for both Customers and Experts. CSMs are employees of EASWORKS who work with Experts and Customers to ensure projects, Customers, and Experts are successful.

In order to become a EASWORKS contractor, you must have strong spoken and written English skills, a fast and reliable computer and internet connection, willingness to adjust to clients’ working schedules, high-quality work, a positive and professional attitude, and a desire to constantly learn and improve yourself.

Working with Global Talent

Once your profile is complete, you may be invited to apply for roles that match up with the "Skills" section of your profile and your time zone. If your application is thorough and relevant to the role, you may be invited to your first interview with the EASWORKS Talent team. This first interview helps us get to know you better - we'll discuss your work experience and how it is relevant to this specific role. If you pass the first interview, there will be a second “technical” interview conducted by a member of our technical team. In this meeting, you will be asked about the uses of specific technologies and will likely be asked to do a coding challenge while sharing your screen. If you pass our vetting process, you will be presented to the client. If you are presented, the client will be able to see your public profile and request to interview you. Each client has their own approach to the interview process, so further instructions will be provided to you prior to your client interview.

Before you choose a project, a project chooses you. Each time a Customer requests a project or flexible talent position, a EASWORKS Customer Success Manager (CSM) initiates a Talent Matching process to make the best match between Customer and Talent. Once the CSM notifies you that you’re a Talent Match, you have the option to accept or pass on the contract – the contract will include deliverables, expectations, and payment details. Once you accept the contract, you’ll get started with the Customer’s project.

EASWORKS generally pays out in 1 of 3 ways: Hourly tracked payments in bi-weekly cadence, 50-Down/50-Done, or 100% upon completion.
