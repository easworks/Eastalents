Intellectual Property

The client. EASWORKS only business is connecting world-class Enterprise Application experts to the clients who want to work with them - not intellectual property. Our contracts provide that all work created by a EASWORKS expert is the property of the client, not EASWORKS or Talent.

We take your intellectual property issues very seriously. EASWORKS is happy to get a mutual non-disclosure agreement in place with you before discussing the details of your project. We have strict confidentiality agreements in place with our contractors, and IP is never owned by any freelancer but don’t hold yourself back from signing MSA or any other necessary documents with the developer if you wish.

EASWORKS enters into talent agreements with every member of the talent network. Through these agreements, EASWORKS obtains exclusive ownership of the work created by the EASWORKS talent that works with you. We then provide you with the intellectual property for the work that the EASWORKS talent creates for you under our client agreement. This clean, simple, pass-through arrangement has worked well for our clients for many years. However, if you would like the expert to enter into a direct agreement with you to affect the direct transfer of IP property, we can work with you to ensure that your needs are met.
